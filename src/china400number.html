CHINA 400 NUMBER

China 400 Number serve the entire country, through a single 400 number and are accessible from a mobile phone. Plus, our service provides access to both fixed and mobile users both North and South China.

China 400 Number (China Shared Cost Number)is number that the caller pays for a local call, but the number is accessible from everywhere in the country and can be forwarded to anywhere in the world.

Why should I choose a China 400 Number?

China 400 Number is the most famous marketing tool instead of 800 number in China.They serve all of China, both North and South and can be dialed from mobile phones.In China,mobile-friendly toll free numbers are great for attracting new business to your company because most Chinese use only mobile phones.
